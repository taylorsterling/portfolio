About Me


Hello!

In 2013, I moved from Syracuse, NY to Houston, TX. Here I earned a Business Degree and began doing clerical work and customer service.
My husband, a software engineer, suggested that I consider taking a new career path. When he mentioned software development, it sounded impossible at first. He convinced me by showing me how simple and fun it could be just to try a basic HTML page.

In January of 2018, I decided to make the first big step toward learning to code, and enrolled in the Houston Coding Bootcamp (by UT Austin). It was a fast- paced, grueling, and unbelievably rewarding six month course in the very basics of code. From Bootcamp I not only learned the fundamentals, but I discovered new- found skills and passions in graphics, logic, and teamwork.

New skills coming soon!
